My Work

Enunciate

Research has shown that one of the reasons young people abandon their goal to learn a new language/vocabulary is due to lack of time. Enunciate is an app designed to empower young people to learn new vocabulary by incorporating short lessons into their daily schedules.

QuikPay

Ghana is one of the fasted developing nations in West Africa, but security in the financial transaction system leaves much to be desired. QuikPay is a secure financial web app designed to inspire confidence in the way Ghanaians perform financial transactions.

View Case Study

Dig-IT

Dig-IT is a HelpAge Canada program that offers devices, data, education, and technical support for low-income Canadian seniors to join the digital world.
